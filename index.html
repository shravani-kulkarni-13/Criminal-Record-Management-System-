Kiran Bedi

Kiran Bedi is the first woman IPS officer in India. She joined the Indian Police Service in 1972 and was the 24th Lieutenant Governor of Puducherry.

Ajit Doval

Ajit Doval is the National Security Advisor of India. He was recruited in the Indian Police Service in 1968 in the Kerala cadre.

Sanjukta Parashar

Sanjukta Parashar Known as Iron Lady of Assam.She is the courageous officer who took down 16 militants. She has become a symbol of bravery.

Shivdeep Lande

Shivdeep Waman Lande is 2006 batch IPS officer. He is currently serving as DCP in the Anti-narcotics cell, Crime Branch (Mumbai) in Maharashtra Police.

Safin Hasan

IPS Safin Hasan is the youngest IPS officer of the country.He began his new position as the Assistant Superintendent of Police for District Jamnagar, entering a setting that typifies his tenacious character.

Merin Joseph

IPS officer Merin Joseph embarked on a mission to bring a child-rape accused to justice, transcending international borders. Her relentless pursuit of the fugitive showcased her unwavering commitment towards safety.

Vishwas Nagre-Patil

Vishwas Nangre-Patil, an IPS officer appointed as the Joint Commissioner of Police, Mumbai exemplifies the face a quintessential, competent police officer who has endeared himself to the higher rank and file of his dept.

Soumya Sambasivan

IPS officer Soumya Sambasivan is the first female SP in Shimla, Himachal Pradesh. She is known for solving six murder cases including two blind murder mysteries when she posted in Sirmaur district for two years.
